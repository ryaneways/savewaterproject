About Us

The Save Water Project was founded out of fun and love for water. Our desire is to supply water saving toilets to water lovers. Although we are a company that wishes to save water, we are not here to discuss the negative statistics on Earth's water resources.

We are fun

We prefer not to focus on cloudy days. Whether the statistics are right or wrong is not relevant. We believe that water, along with all of nature's resources, should be respected and used with reserve.

Our toilets

Our toilets on average use one gallon per flush as opposed to five gallons for a normal toilet. This saves 20 gallons of water per day, and 7,000 gallons of water per year.

Our toilets are also attractive. They are based on the design of a master, Philippe Starck. And we believe they invoke a certain elegance that reflects this.
